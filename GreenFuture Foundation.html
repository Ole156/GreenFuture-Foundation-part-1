About Us

Founded in 2015 in Johannesburg by dedicated environmental activists, GreenFuture Foundation works to inspire eco-friendly living through tree planting drives, recycling programs, and community-based environmental workshops.

Community-driven tree planting events across schools, parks, and neighbourhoods.
