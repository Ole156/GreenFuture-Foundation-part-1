About GreenFuture Foundation

Founded in 2015 by environmental activists in Johannesburg, GreenFuture Foundation advocates for sustainable living, tree planting, and community environmental education.

Mission

To promote sustainable practices through community action, education, and project delivery.

Vision

A greener, healthier future where communities lead environmental stewardship.
