About Us

GreenFuture Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to promoting sustainability, protecting the environment, and empowering communities. Founded in 2015, we started as a small group of volunteers planting trees in local neighborhoods. Over the years, we’ve grown into a recognized foundation working on projects across renewable energy, conservation, and waste reduction.

Our Mission: To create a greener, cleaner, and healthier world through action, awareness, and education.

Our Vision: A future where every community thrives in harmony with nature.

We partner with schools, local businesses, and global environmental networks to expand our reach and maximize impact.
